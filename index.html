What is Helping Paws?

Helping Paws is the volunteering side of Furosion. This meetup is your chance to meet the team, explore roles, and see how events come together. Come learn, connect, and decide if you want to join the team!

What You'll Do

At this meetup, you'll:

  • Explore volunteer roles and responsibilities
  • Meet organisers and ask questions
  • See behind the scenes of event planning
  • Decide if you’d like to join our team

This is the only volunteer info session—we don’t run multiple signups. It’s your chance to get a feel for volunteering with us.

What to Expect

People from the Furosion organisation team
Someone to guide you through volunteer roles
Answers to your questions about volunteering
Snacks, water, and a chill area to relax
A small presentation room with comfy seats
Note: This is a small community space, not a professional venue. Keep expectations casual!
